About

This is my website where I write movie reviews about films that I have watched. Movies are an interest of mine because it's fun to immerse myself in a different world and another character's narrative. After watching movies, I tend to have many thoughts I want to share about what I just watched.
I hope you can find this movie reviews I leave here helpful and informative. There are a lot of movies that I loved and that I wished more people could watch and enjoy as well. In addition, there are a lot of bad movies that I hope I can save people from having to watch. At the end of the day, these reviews are simply my own opinion, so please keep that in mind!
